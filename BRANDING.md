# AgentMedha Brand Guidelines

## Name & Meaning

### Primary Name
**AgentMedha**

### Pronunciation
**AY-jent MAY-dha** or **AY-jent MEH-dha**

### Etymology
- **Medha** (Sanskrit: मेधा): Intelligence, wisdom, mental power
- **Medha Devi**: Hindu goddess of intelligence, aspect of Saraswati
- **Agent**: AI agent/system

### Tagline Options

**Primary**: *"Intelligence meets data"*

**Alternatives**:
- "Wisdom from your data"
- "AI-powered analytics intelligence"
- "Empowering intelligence through data"
- "Where intelligence meets insights"

## Voice & Tone

### Brand Personality

**Intelligent** - We're smart, but not arrogant  
**Accessible** - Complex made simple  
**Empowering** - We enable, not replace  
**Trustworthy** - Reliable, secure, honest  
**Modern** - Cutting-edge but proven  

### Writing Style

**Do**:
- ✅ Use clear, simple language
- ✅ Explain technical concepts simply
- ✅ Be helpful and empowering
- ✅ Show, don't just tell
- ✅ Use active voice

**Don't**:
- ❌ Use unnecessary jargon
- ❌ Be condescending
- ❌ Overpromise
- ❌ Use buzzwords without meaning
- ❌ Be too formal or stiff

### Example Messages

**Good**:
> "AgentMedha turns your questions into insights in seconds. Just ask in plain English."

**Bad**:
> "Leverage AgentMedha's synergistic AI paradigm to facilitate data-driven decision optimization."

## Cultural Sensitivity

### Using the Sanskrit Name

**Do**:
- ✅ Acknowledge the cultural origin
- ✅ Explain the meaning when introducing
- ✅ Pronounce correctly (MAY-dha or MEH-dha)
- ✅ Honor the goddess Medha Devi's significance
- ✅ Use respectfully in all contexts

**Don't**:
- ❌ Appropriate without acknowledgment
- ❌ Use religious imagery inappropriately
- ❌ Mispronounce consistently without correction
- ❌ Use in disrespectful contexts

### Acknowledgment Text

When introducing the name:
> "AgentMedha—named after Medha Devi, the Hindu goddess of intelligence and wisdom, an aspect of Saraswati."
